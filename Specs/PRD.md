# Share, Instead. Product Requirements Document

## Overview
Share, Instead. is a community-focused platform designed to foster local connection and sustainability. It allows neighbors to share and borrow items (tools, books, equipment), trade homegrown produce, and offer community services.

## Core Features
1.  **Item Sharing & Borrowing**: A local marketplace for non-monetary item lending.
2.  **Surplus Exchange**: A dedicated space for sharing garden surplus (eggs, vegetables, fruits).
3.  **Local Services**: A community bartering system for skills like gardening help, tech support, etc.
4.  **Community Circles**: Groups based on neighborhood proximity or shared interests.

## Target Audience
-   Environmentally conscious individuals looking to reduce waste.
-   Budget-conscious neighbors who prefer borrowing over buying.
-   Community-oriented people looking to connect with those living nearby.

---

## Design Vision
The design reflects "The Modern Commons"—a warm, editorial, and sophisticated interface that feels trustworthy and friendly.
-   **Primary Palette**: Forest Green (#2e7d32), Sunset Orange (#ff9800), Cream White (#fffdfa).
-   **Typography**: Lexend (Rounded, friendly).
